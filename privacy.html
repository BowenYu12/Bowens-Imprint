Usage – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Usage ("the app") is a third-party, read-only tool that displays your Codex usage status. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI.

Data We Collect

None. Usage has no server. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no user accounts.

Your Credential

To read your usage status, you paste a credential generated by the Codex CLI on your own computer. This credential is stored exclusively in your device's Keychain (system-level encryption). It never leaves your device except for direct requests from your device to the official service endpoints (chatgpt.com / auth.openai.com) in order to read your usage status and refresh the credential. The developer has no access to it.

Usage History

The app keeps a small local history of your usage percentages (for the trend chart) in the app's private container on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Widgets

Lock screen and home screen widgets read the same locally stored data and make the same direct requests from your device. No third party is involved.

Children's Privacy

The app is a developer utility and does not target children. Since no data is collected, no children's data is collected either.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new date.
